Julia Martin

Bio

Julia Martin is a first year Biostatistics Masters student at the Johns Hopkins University Bloomberg School of Public Health. She graduated from UCLA in June 2024 with a Bachelor of Science in Statistics and Data Science and a Minor in Public Health. While studying at UCLA, she was a student researcher at the UCLA Department of Medicine Statistics Core, in which she assisted a senior statistician in data analysis on patterns in childhood trauma using R. Her research interests include clinical trials and causal inference. In this course, she is curious to learn how to use Git to manage coding and data files.
